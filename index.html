The Problem
Good leads. No system.
Follow-up dies after 2 calls.
01
Follow-up falls off
Agents make 2–3 attempts and move on. Most sales happen at touch 5–8. Without a sequence engine, those leads just die in a spreadsheet.
02
No manager visibility
With 10 agents and no centralized system, you have no real pipeline visibility. You can't see who's performing, where leads are falling off, or what's driving results.
03
New agents have no clear playbook
New agents don't know who to call, what to say, or when. Without qualified direction, they burn through good leads and lose confidence fast.

The Solution
One app. One screen.
One Agency.
The Pulse
Every morning, each agent sees the one lead to act on right now. AI-scored from your Meta leads, ranked by intent. No guesswork even for brand new agents.
Auto Sequences
Every lead gets a structured follow-up sequence, automatically staged and ready to send. Agents stay consistent without thinking about it. One tap to approve nothing goes out without their sign-off.
Manager Dashboard
See every agent's pipeline in real time. Who's working, who's stalling, where deals are dying. Full visibility across all 10 states no more spreadsheet guessing.
Built-in Compliance
TCPA windows, DNC checks, all 10 licensed states verified before every touch. New agents can't make a compliance mistake even if they try.
